About Hemophilia

Empowering patients through safe, guided, and consistent movement.

🩸 What is Hemophilia?

Hemophilia is a rare bleeding disorder where blood doesn't clot properly. This can lead to internal bleeding, most commonly in the joints and muscles, which can cause long-term damage if not managed.

💪 Why Physiotherapy?

Building strong muscles around the joints acts as a natural "shock absorber," protecting them from everyday wear and tear and reducing the frequency of spontaneous joint bleeds.

⚠️ Safety First

Exercises for hemophilia patients must be low-impact and precise. Pushing too hard or using incorrect form can trigger a bleed rather than prevent one.
