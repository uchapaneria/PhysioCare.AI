Advanced, low-impact physiotherapy guidance for hemophilia patients, protected by real-time AI movement tracking.

Why This Platform Matters

For individuals living with hemophilia, maintaining joint health is not just about staying fit—it is a critical necessity. Without strong muscles to support joints like the knees, elbows, and ankles, patients are at a much higher risk of spontaneous, painful internal bleeds.

However, performing physical therapy incorrectly can sometimes cause the very injuries you are trying to prevent.

PhysioCare bridges the gap between clinical visits and at-home exercises. We provide specialized, low-impact routines and pair them with cutting-edge AI technology that watches your form. This ensures every stretch and bend is safe, precise, and effective.
